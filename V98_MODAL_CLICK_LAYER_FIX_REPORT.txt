V98 MODAL CLICK LAYER FIX

Cause found:
- V95 set .modal-shade to z-index:99990.
- Settings modal still had normal modal z-index.
- The shade was visually dimming the screen and intercepting taps above the Settings panel.
- The Typography button was visible through the shade but not clickable.

Fix:
- Removed V95/V96 diagnostic modal z-index blocks.
- Corrected layer order:
  app/dock < modal shade < settings/game/rounds sheets < typography panel
- Kept Typography button high in Settings.
- Removed green diagnostic panel styling.
