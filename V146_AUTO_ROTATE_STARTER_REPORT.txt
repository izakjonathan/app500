V146 Auto-Rotate Starter

Built from V145.

Changed:
- Starter now automatically moves to the next player after every successful Add Round.
- The saved round still records who started that round.
- The top Starter button still works as a manual override before adding a round.
- Rotation follows the current player order and wraps back to the first player.

Example:
- Round 1 starter: You
- Tap Add Round
- Next round starter automatically becomes GF
- Tap Add Round
- Next round starter automatically becomes Player 3, etc.

No scoring/sync/multi-game logic changed.
